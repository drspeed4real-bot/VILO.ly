Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

At VILO.LY, we respect your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect when you play our games or use our site, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: Account name, email, password, support messages, and any content you submit in-game.

Automatic information: IP address, device type, browser, game progress, scores, crash reports, and cookies. See our Cookies Policy.

Information from others: If you log in via Google, Discord, or Facebook, we receive your basic profile info from them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and improve our games and services
  • Save your progress and preferences
  • Provide player support and respond to you
  • Prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse
  • Show personalized ads, with your consent where required
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers: Hosting, analytics, ad networks, and support tools that help us run VILO.LY
  • Legal reasons: If required by law or to protect our players
  • Business transfers: If we’re acquired or merge with another company

4. Data Retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. Game analytics data is kept for up to 24 months. You can request deletion anytime.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, email privacy@vilo.ly
