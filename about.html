VILO.LY is an independent game studio from Al Khums, Libya. We build fast, fun, browser-first games for players worldwide.

Our Story

VILO.LY started in 2024 with one mission: bring back the magic of instant, no-download gaming. We were tired of 100GB updates and 20-minute load screens.

So we made games that load in seconds and hook you in minutes. No installs, no paywalls, just pure gameplay. From arcade shooters to puzzle battlers, if it’s fun, we’ll ship it. And we’re just getting started.

What We Believe

Gameplay first: Graphics are cool, but mechanics are king. If it’s not fun in 10 seconds, it’s not done.

Respect your time: No grindy tutorials, no forced logins. Click play and you’re in.

Made for everyone: Our games run on phones, laptops, and that old PC in your library. No one gets left out.
