Cookies Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

VILO.LY uses cookies and similar technologies to run our games, remember your preferences, and understand how players use our site. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences over time. We also use local storage, pixels, and similar tech for the same purposes.

2. How We Use Cookies

Type Purpose Example
Essential Required for the site and games to function. Cannot be disabled. Login sessions, game save states, load balancing
Performance Help us measure traffic and fix bugs. Google Analytics, error tracking
Functionality Remember your choices to give you a better experience. Language, volume settings, theme preference
Advertising Used to show relevant ads and limit how often you see them. Google AdSense, in-game ad networks

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by services we use:

  • Google Analytics - Understand player behavior
  • Google AdSense - Serve and measure ads
  • Cloudflare - Security and performance
  • Discord - Community login widget

These third parties have their own privacy policies.

4. Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will break game saves and login. For advertising cookies, you can opt out via YourOnlineChoices or About Ads.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our games and services change. We’ll post the new date above.

Questions about cookies? Email us at privacy@vilo.ly.
